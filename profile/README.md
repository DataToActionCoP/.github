# Global Public Health Data Science Community of Practice

Public-health threats frequently cross-national borders. Our vision is to establish an international community of practice (CoP) where public-health data scientists from the United States, the European Union, East Asia, and Southeast Asia collaborate in a common GitHub environment. By working together, we can share public datasets, analytic code, AI/ML models, documentation, and best practices to accelerate digital health innovations.

## Our Objectives

* **Create a shared repository ecosystem** on GitHub to host public datasets, synthetic datasets, standardized metadata, data dictionaries, and open-source information.

* **Facilitate collaborative development** of AI/ML models for public-health problems, such as outbreak detection and disease forecasting.

* **Develop and disseminate best-practice guidelines** on data anonymization, FAIR compliance, and regulatory compliance across jurisdictions.

* **Build capacity and networks** by organizing webinars, hackathons, training modules, and mentorship programs across regions.

* **Influence policy and standards** by engaging with regulators and global initiatives to align community practices with evolving interoperability standards.
